# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a welcoming, safe, and respectful community for everyone,
regardless of background, identity, experience level, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, constructive, and specific.
- Critique ideas and work rather than people.
- Respect differing perspectives and experience levels.
- Accept feedback and correct mistakes in good faith.
- Protect private, confidential, and security-sensitive information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, stalking, sexualized conduct, sustained
disruption, personal attacks, and publishing another person's private
information without permission are not tolerated.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews,
and other project spaces, as well as when someone represents the project in a
public community.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through a maintainer contact channel published
by the repository. If no private channel is available, ask maintainers to create
one without posting sensitive details publicly.

Maintainers will review reports as impartially and confidentially as practical.
Depending on severity and context, they may edit or remove content, issue a
warning, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently remove someone from
project spaces. Retaliation against a reporter or participant in an
investigation is itself a violation.
